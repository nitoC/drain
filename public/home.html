    Build Apps That Connect Across Chains

    Moonbeam is a platform for cross-chain connected applications that unites assets and functionality from many blockchains.

    Connect Wallet

    Reinvigorating the Pace of Innovation

    Moonbeam powers better experiences, using Web3 principles to propel application development to the next level.

    As a smart contract platform for building cross-chain connected applications, Moonbeam powers dApps that can access users, assets, and services on any chain.

    Connectivity at Our Core

    Moonbeam gives you the agility to move with the market and tap into endless resources from Polkadot, Polygon, Ethereum — and beyond. Combine the functionality of multiple specialized chains or simply deploy natively on Moonbeam to bring your project to new heights.

    Unmatched Cross-Chain Smart Contract Capabilities

    Use Moonbeam’s Connected Contracts to unite your multi-chain dapp deployments into one impeccable user experience.

    Layer One with Fast Finality & Secure Scaling

    Shared security and finality derived from the Polkadot Relay Chain gives Moonbeam’s platform the full strength of Polkadot’s validator set.

    Your Favorite Tools & Programming Language

    Builders on Moonbeam can tap into the most popular Ethereum-based developer tools, accounts, wallets, and more.

    Access to Users, Assets, and Resources on Any Chain

    Use Moonbeam smart contracts as the "brain" of your multi-chain deployment, so you can flex with the demands of the market and seamlessly expand to reach new users.

    Connectivity on Moonbeam

    Grow Your Project with Multi-Chain

    Uniting the Best of Web3 in One dApp Development Paradise

    New Users and Use Cases

    Lower gas fees open the door to innovative projects, attracting a diverse user base and providing cost-effective solutions for businesses.

    Rich Interoperability

    Gain immediate access to resources on other blockchains, both in the Polkadot ecosystem and outside of it via Axelar, Wormhole and LayerZero.

    Growing Ecosystem

    Join the hundreds of applications, projects, and tools that live on Moonbeam’s rapidly expanding ecosystem.
